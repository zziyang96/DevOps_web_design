Co-founder and Chef Cong Trinh


Rice-Flour Crust Recipe
Tradition since 2018

One of your co-founder, Cong Trinh, has long been passionate in culinary since he was young. His passion led him into researching and experimenting in mixing different and sometimes polar opposite cuisines into new and exciting to tastebuds dishes while emphasizing on healthy side of his dishes.

Cong is from country of Vietnam in South East Asia where cooks primary focused on creating healthy and well-balanced meals. For example, pho – worldwide famous noodle soups which have a great balance between meat, vegetable, broth, and rice flour noodle. During his teen years, he had a chance to travel to origin country of pizza, Italy. He was mesmerized by Italian pizza masters and by the diversity of ingredients included in cooking that has pushed original pizza, Neapolitan pizza, to evolve into many different styles of pizza like world famous deep dish pizza or Chicago pizza and New York style pizza. Coming back home, Cong has started his long path with his two co-founders in creating what we call Pizza Inc. His dream became to not only be an Italian pizza master, but also to surpass them by creating a new type of pizza at would be of taste to anyone of any age.

Throughout the many years of trials, experiments, failures, and successes, one of Cong’s idea is to substitute traditional wheat flour with rice based flour which is not only delicious, but also gluten-free which would allow so many people to enjoy the wonders of pizza. A clean, delicious, and also gluten free pizza crust recipe can be extremely hard to find these days. Most recipes contain added gums, such as guar or xanthan gum which can be hard on the digestive system, particularly for those with low stomach acid. Additionally, most paleo pizza crusts are made with tapioca starch, almond flour, arrowroot, or potato starch which can also be problematic digestively.

Co-founder and Chef Victor She


An intergration of West and East way
Variety of charms and attractive

One of your co-founders, Victor she, lives in China. One day, a friend of Victor opened a pizza shop and invited him to visit. Ever since he took a mouthful of chicken and vegetable pizza, he has fallen in love with pizza. But he thinks pizza's flavor is limited to western elements too monotonous. So he wondered if we could combine the two different elements of food culture from the East and the West and create some new pizzas.

Based on this idea, Victor, he wondered if we could mix two seemingly unrelated food elements from two different countries and make a new pizza, like rice pizza. Rice is a common staple food in Eastern China, pizza is also a common staple food in the West. In keeping with the traditional pizza making style, get rid of the traditional chicken, beef and other food elements, and add new elements of the East that have not been tested, such as replacing the traditional western chicken and beef with Chinese rice and roast duck as the main material. Figs or fermented bean curd replace the traditional western cheese and tomato sause as a seasoning. Through the combination of multiple elements, create more creative new dishes, stimulate our taste buds, so that customers will always have a fresh experience.

Co-founder and Chef Aneesh Antal aka Batman


Indian Flavors
Our Chef Story and Background

Aneesh Antãl (born 15 October 1993) is Indian chief and co-founder of Pizza Inc. Born in Chandigarh, India, most of his early life he work and studied technology. Growing up in Chandigarh, India, Aneesh developed skills in information technology from Punjab Technical University and entrepreneurship early on. He and his father built a snack truck selling special family recipe of stuffed samosa curry buns when he was 16 years old. Working with his father, Aneesh found his passion in cooking. Aneesh eventually earned enough money from selling pretzels to fund the adventure of a lifetime; at the age of 21, Aneesh spent studying in La Cuisine Paris, France, which furthered his culinary education.

In 2000s, while attending the La Cuisine Paris, France, Aneesh worked several different restaurant jobs. He even served as a flambé captain for a time. After earning his degree in hospitality management, Aneesh continued working in restaurants. He put his degree to work at a Stouffer's restaurant where he focused on French cuisine. However, he never felt that it French cuisine was something he wanted to leave as his legacy and began experimenting with fusing different cuisines.

His culinary path has lead him to DC, where he began is working as sous chef at Sfoglina, an Italian restaurant. Working there, Aneesh met Cong Trinh and Victor Zhen who just like Aneesh wanted to chance the entire culinary industry through innovation. Leaving Sfoglina, three co-founders of Pizza. Inc continuously worked on experimenting to create an amazing menu of what we call now Pizza. Inc.
